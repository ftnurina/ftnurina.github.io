about us

Since we launched our first location in 2021, Fatyinn Restaurant in Setia Alam, we have been immensely passionate about providing patrons with locally crafted artisan meals and beverages. Our goal is to provide both a superior customer experience with prohibition-era entertainment and tremendous value for our customers. Only the taste is expensive. Additionally, we have other outlets in the Bandar Baru Bangi areas.

Customers have the option of dining in the establishment, as well as placing an order online through our in-house delivery service, GrabFood, FoodPanda, or ShopeeFood.

We are grateful for all of our customers and value the feedback and recommendations they provide. Send us an email at fatyinnrest@gmail.com and tell us what you think we're doing well as well as where we can make improvements.
